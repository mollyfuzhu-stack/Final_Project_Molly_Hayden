# Final_Project_Molly_Hayden

The data set we are wokring on is from an acclimation exposurure with Killi fish. Killi fish were raised in two conditions, 10 and 24 degrees and in salinity conditions of 11 ppt or 28 ppt. They were then put in differnt condtions than what they were raised in to determine the change in mitochondrial enzyme activity and body condition. We plan to do a mulitvariate analaysis to determine how the differnt acclimation conditions impact enzyme activity and body condition. The predictors are temperature and salinity acclimation. The response variables are COX activity, CS activity, LDH activity, Heatoctrit, and body condition.
